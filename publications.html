Book chapter

  1. Michael J. Neely and Hao Yu, ‘‘Lagrangian Methods for Convergence in Constrained Convex Programs” Convex Optimization: Theory, Methods, and Applications, edited by Arto Ruud, Nova Publishers, Jan. 2019. [PDF file]. This book chapter serves as an educational medium to teach our O(1/T) constrained convex programming method developed in our SIOPT2017 paper and related Lagrangian methods. For the educational purpose, Mike even designed a couple of exercise problems.
