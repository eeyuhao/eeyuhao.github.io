About me

I am an applied scientist at Amazon. Before joining Amazon, I was a machine learning researcher in Alibaba (DAMO Academy, Machine Intelligence Technology Team).

I received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering Department at University of Southern California (USC). My Ph.D. advisor is Prof. Michael J. Neely. Research interests

My research focuses on developing efficient algorithms for large scale optimization (e.g., training deep neural networks), stochastic network optimization (e.g., resource allocation, scheduling, delay and congestion control in networks) and online learning (e.g., statistical, adversarial or bandit learning) in decision making.
